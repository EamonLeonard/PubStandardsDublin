What is it?

Pub Standards Dublin is the largest monthly meetup of developers, designers, founders and
people-who-like-to-build-stuff, in Ireland. It has been running in London and other cities since 2005, and in Dublin since August 2010.

Pub Standards is organised by Eamon Leonard & Ross Duggan of Engine Yard.

Who is it for?

Pub Standards is open to everyone. It's loosely aimed at web` design & development geeks, but that doesn't mean chitchat need necessarily be work-related.  Don't expect structure, don't expect presentations, just relax with likeminded people and a few beers.


This is the post-conference drink-up, without the conference

Where?

The Bull & Castle, beside Christ Church, Dublin City. You'll find us in the beer hall upstairs.

Chat

Between PubStandards, we chat on IRC - we're on Freenode at #pubstandardsdub

When?

The second Thursday of every month, 18:30 onwards.
